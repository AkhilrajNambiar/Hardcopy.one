What is Hardcopy?

This is a website that will offer you used,quality hardcopy of books for free! Yes, the books won't be ebooks, and all those who complain, that they are not able to read for long hours in ebooks, then this website is absolutely for them.

The only thing you got to pay for is the delivery charge which is 40₹ for Indian residents, and only 2$ in case of every other reader outside India. Now I hope it won't be repelling you from using this, as the delivery service needs payment from me!

Easily buy whatever book you want, by just clicking on it and basically for free. Uploading books is also free for all you wonderful people, and do not at all hesitate in uploading the books, before they lose out on quality!
