Purpose

Just to help all of you, yes you who is reading this, to help you to freely obtain the hardcopy of any book you like literally for free!
Used books which satisfy a set of quality values(Mentioned over here), will be offered to you with very little hustles and for free!!


System

If you are curious how Hardcopy works, then just know that I am a facilitator for all of you to offer your used books and to obtain used books for free! (quality will be maintained be rest assured!)
The email address of the donor will be shared with the recepient so you can easily communicate.

FAQs(Frequently Asked Questions)

Why should I pay 40₹ as a fee?

Well, just because I have to pay the delivery service which I am partnering with. I assure you I don't take any extra cost!

What about books with MRP less than 40₹?

5₹ delivery charge for all books that have MRP below 40₹

Yes, of course! You can sign up to avoid filling up the address section every time you order a book.
